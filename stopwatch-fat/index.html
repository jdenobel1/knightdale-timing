    Directions

    Use the following buttons for the stopwatch:

    • Start Stopwatch: Starts the timer manually.
    • Reset Stopwatch: Resets the stopwatch to 00:00:00.000.
    • Record Video: Starts recording the video along with the stopwatch.
    • Stop Recording & Download: Stops the recording and saves the video as an MP4.
    • CTRL + SHIFT: Exports logged times as a CSV file.
    • Spacebar: Logs the current stopwatch time and adds a bookmark to the video.
